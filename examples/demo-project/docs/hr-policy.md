# HR Policy Sample

## Remote Work Approval

Employees may request remote work for up to three days per week. A request should include the employee name, team, requested days, work location, and manager approval.

Remote work is not automatically approved. Managers should consider team coverage, customer support needs, data security requirements, and whether the role can be performed remotely.

## Expense Reimbursement

Expense reimbursement requests should be submitted within 30 calendar days of the purchase date. Each request should include a receipt, business purpose, cost center, and manager approval.

Approved reimbursements are usually processed within 10 business days after finance review.

## Equipment

Company laptops must use disk encryption and screen lock. Employees should report lost or damaged equipment to IT within one business day.
