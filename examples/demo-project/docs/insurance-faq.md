# Insurance FAQ Sample

## Deductible

A deductible is the amount a member pays before the insurance plan starts sharing covered costs.

## Coinsurance

Coinsurance is the percentage of covered costs paid by the member after the deductible has been met.

## Claim Review

A claim review may require the invoice, proof of payment, service date, provider name, and a short explanation of the service.

Most routine claim reviews are completed within 15 business days after all required documents are received.

## Exclusions

This sample FAQ does not describe a real policy. Plan exclusions, limits, and eligibility rules must be checked against the official policy document.
