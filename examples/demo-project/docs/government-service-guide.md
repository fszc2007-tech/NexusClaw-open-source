# Government Service Guide Sample

## Appointment Booking

Residents should prepare an identity document, contact phone number, service category, preferred appointment date, and any supporting documents listed by the service office.

Appointments can usually be changed before the scheduled time. Late changes may require a new booking.

## Address Update

An address update request should include the applicant name, previous address, new address, effective date, and proof of address.

Examples of proof of address include utility bills, bank statements, tenancy agreements, or official letters issued within the last three months.

## Facility Maintenance

Urgent facility maintenance issues should be reported with the location, issue type, photo if available, and contact information for follow-up.
