# Medical Billing EOB Sample

## Explanation Of Benefits

An Explanation of Benefits, or EOB, summarizes how a claim was processed. It may show the billed amount, allowed amount, deductible, coinsurance, copay, plan payment, and member responsibility.

## Member Responsibility

Member responsibility is the amount the member may need to pay after the plan processes the claim. It can include deductible, coinsurance, copay, or non-covered charges.

## Common Review Questions

When reviewing a bill, compare the provider invoice with the EOB. Check the service date, provider name, billed amount, allowed amount, plan payment, and member responsibility.

If the invoice amount does not match the EOB, the member may need to contact the provider billing office or the plan administrator.
